On successful registration and verification are you ready to take challenge; then go the lobby and select the match from current and upcoming series. We have wide coverage of International, Domestic and T20 Cricket Tournaments.

Create your playwin team with 11 players combinations of wicket keeper, batsman, bowler and all rounder with in budget of 100 credits. We are not restricting you to select player with only certain combinations you have to just follow minimum and maximum criteria. You can Check players information by clicking on the pic of the player to take review of his career history and to decide whether to include him in your team or not.

Skillfully Select your Playwin Captain and Vice captain from selected 11 players and avail benefit to receive 2 times the point of Captains performance and 1.5 times the point of Vice Captains Performance. Please check our playwinfantasy point table to get know some more categories.

You can create as many teams as you want for respective match and choose to join a contests with any of these created teams. Playwinfantasy gives you one more chance to edit your team along with captain and vice captain until the deadline of the match.

Your Playwinfantasy team accumulates the score for their real performance based on fantasy point attributed to the various action of the players on the field.

Please check our fantasy cricket point system chart through which you can decide your strategy to select the combinations of players to get higher rank. It is platform where you can confirm the total score you received for the respective match.

Fantasy score for the players will be update as per the ICC rule. If match get interrupted by rain or the result get decided by D/L method; players will be awarded points. If match get abandoned no points will be awarded.

My Contest section will show you the details of points, rank you achieved and amount of prize you won in respective contest you joined. This section will give you the details of the team chosen by your opponent also but only after the respective contest match gets start. Ranks and points will be updated and displayed during the match in real time.

The winner declaration and crediting of the winnings to your playwinfantasy account usually happens within a hour after the match is completed.

Winners rank shall be decided on the on the basis of highest scoring of points. If on any rank the tie happens for the score the winner prize for that respective rank shall be given to the player who has entered into contest earlier. The rest of the player with same score shall get the prize for the next rank and so on.

Your playwinfantasy account shall be update shortly after the match. On dashboard you will get summary of your accounts. You can check the detail ledger of respective head in transaction section.
